## 5. Legend
- **β (Beta)**: The percentage of total cycle time consumed by this function.
- **μ (Mu)**: The average utilization or counter value of an associated resource during the function's execution.
- **Device**: The device associated with the metric (e.g., GPU ID, CPU core).
- **Z-Score(β/log_μ)**: A measure of the statistical significance of a change. A higher absolute value indicates a more unusual change.
- **p-value/U-test p**: The p-value from a statistical test. Lower values (e.g., < 0.05) suggest the difference is statistically significant.
- **Suspicion Score**: A composite score `|Δβ| * (|Z-Score(β)| + |Z-Score(log_μ)|)` to rank potential issues. **Focus on items with the highest score first.**
